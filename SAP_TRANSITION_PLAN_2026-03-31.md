## Goal

Build a credible 6-week transition from business analysis and product ownership into SAP-adjacent roles in Germany, with the fastest path focused on:

- SAP Signavio
- SAP Activate
- SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition process and project understanding
- process-heavy SAP roles, not deep module-specialist roles yet

This plan is optimized for your current background:

- 10+ years in BA / PO / PM / product delivery
- strong fit in requirements, stakeholder alignment, workflows, rollout, onboarding, process improvement
- currently weak in hands-on SAP module experience

That means the best first move is not FI/CO, MM, SD, PP, HCM, or other deep module specialization. The best first move is to position yourself as:

`Business Analyst / Product Owner / Process Consultant transitioning into SAP through Signavio, SAP Activate, and S/4HANA business-process learning in Germany.`
