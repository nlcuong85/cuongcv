## How to Evaluate a Posting Fast

Green flags:

- Signavio
- business processes
- transformation
- enablement
- project support
- PMO
- change management
- stakeholder management
- workshop support
- rollout
- documentation
- Cloud ALM
- process mining

Yellow flags:

- S/4HANA role but module depth not clearly required
- German desired but not absolutely mandatory
- asks for 1 to 2 years SAP experience but work scope is support-oriented

Red flags:

- `sehr gut Deutsch` or `verhandlungssicher Deutsch` as hard requirement
- years of customizing in FI/CO, MM, SD, PP
- strong ABAP / technical configuration requirements
- senior ownership of SAP roadmap without prior SAP experience
