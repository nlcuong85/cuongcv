# Authentic Writing Samples (2012-2019)

This file stores representative raw writing samples shared directly by Le Cuong Nguyen in chat and used as a long-term reference for voice calibration.

Purpose:
- preserve pre-AI authentic writing
- give future agents direct access to old real wording, rhythm, and motivation patterns
- support both the blog-writer skill and the application system

Important:
- this is a raw archive, not a quality template
- preserve the human signals, not the grammar mistakes or outdated business-letter formulas
- do not copy old phrasing literally into modern applications

## Sample 8: Ninja Network / Shuriken product notes (2018-era concept draft)

```text
The Platform Token

The token of the Ninja network is called Shuriken. Users earn Shuriken by engaging with the platform and being a good neighbor. That’s all there is to it.

In this P2P, modern-day barter trade society, you are able to trade your Shurikens in for more advanced weapons. These will unlock special privileges, features and skills that you can use to enhance your experience on the Ninja network.

Weapons

Winged boots
This gives you access.
Owning a pair of winged boots makes you the first to hear about bounties, airdrops, lucky draws. Contribute to the active growth of the platform by being the first to test and suggest new features.

Nunchaku
This gives you creator’s rights.
Holding this weapon allows you to create markets, orders, predictions. You need this to set your own fees and your own terms.

Stealth cat ring
This gives you visibility.
If you have the stealth cat ring, your exchanges/trades/services are the first ones other ninjas see. This increases engagement and makes it much easier for you to find someone to play with.

Bow and Arrow
This gives you freedom from limits.
Ninjas who do not have this weapon are subject to typical transaction limits that ensure the health of the platform. If you have the bow and arrow, these do not apply to you.

Blow Dart
This increases your luck.
The blow dart is a sneaky one. Holders of the blow dart find that they get lucky more than the average ninja. That’s all we can say.

Knife arsenal
These allow you to slash fees.
There are 3 knives in the arsenal. The humble throwing knife gives you a fee discount of 50%. The kama gives you 75% off, and the rare katana completely erases all fees.

Reputation

In this nobody network of everybody, reputation is extremely important. Since you can’t see someone’s face or check their credentials, you must judge them on the colour of their Zukin.

If you are the sort to play dirty - spammers and scammers we’re looking at you, your Zukin will be downgraded. If you have a good track record of making honourable transactions, your Zukin will reflect this.

Every Ninja starts at Plain white cotton.

The most dishonourable Ninja wears a toilet paper zukin.

The most experienced, wise, and honourable Ninjas wear the black silk zukin.

Thoughts:

Start with giving people free money to bet (what about gas? fees?)
Introduce the shuriken by using it as an entry ticket -> shuriken for gas fees? Everyone gets free shurikens.
Shurikens can now be accumulated and used to trade for weapons that give you additional benefits / features

WEAPONS TRADE

Auction-style Darwinism

Each weapon has an initial price that the first ninjas will sell it for. Let’s say Katana - 100 shurikens.

After this initial sale (how many katanas will we sell at the fixed price of 100?), market forces will determine the price of the item. The person who now holds the Katana is free to set his price - say 500 shurikens. Anyone who is willing to pay that is able to take the Katana away from him at any time. The catch - you pay X% property tax on weapons, so you don’t want to be outrageous.

Thoughts:

- Do we want to put a limit on the number of weapons a ninja can fit in his backpack?
- There are going to be people that just collect weapons and hold them at high prices, in hopes of eventually selling for ETH.
- If people actively want to sell weapons, should there be a public list they can be on?

Marketplace alternative:

In addition to the obvious selling of a weapon in exchange for shuriken,

I can give away my weapon to anyone I want. At that point, they can set the value at which they are willing to part with it.

I can also loan my weapon for a fraction of the price. You can borrow my Katana this week for 10 shuriken, but you have to give me 10% of your winnings.
```

Team I owned at Vintech - 2019.

Trải qua nhiều đau thương mất mát là nền tảng vững chắc cho sự thành công của PO/PM.

Để bắt đầu câu chuyện, tôi đã bắt đầu công việc của mình tại Vintech từ đầu tháng 4 năm 2019 với tư cách là Product Owner cho Cổng đặt phòng (Booking Portal) cho hệ thống khách sạn của Vin.

Thành thật mà nói, đó là một trải nghiệm xấu (cũng có chút tốt) do là vì trong tháng đầu tiên tôi trải qua một đống thông tin quá tải và tôi đã ngất vài lần trong khi làm việc.

PO! you should be the domain expert, it's your role now.

"Họp liên tục ngay sau khi nhận role và ngồi trong cuộc họp tôi chưa hiểu chuyện gì đang xảy ra lúc đó". Đó chính là điều mà tôi trải qua trong 2 tuần đầu tại Vintech.
